This successful supply chain adjustment protects the competitiveness of these products in the global market. While our teams effectively mitigated negative tariff impacts this quarter, tariff rate volatility continues to be a challenge for our operations.

Additionally, in the third quarter, we made the strategic decision to consolidate 4 of our manufacturing plants into 2. Combining facilities allows us to reduce overhead costs, improve direct labor and asset utilization, and enhance the efficiency of our operations. To achieve these consolidation benefits, we are discontinuing a few low-volume, low-margin products.
